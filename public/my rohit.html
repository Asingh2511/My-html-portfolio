About Rohit sharma

Rohit Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of Indian cricket team in Test and ODI formats. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest ODI opening batters of all time.[3] He is a right-handed batsman who plays for Mumbai Indians in Indian Premier League and for Mumbai in domestic cricket. Rohit previously captained India in all three formats

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra, into a Telugu-Marathi speaking family. His father, Gurunath Sharma, worked as a caretaker of a transport firm storehouse, while his mother, Purnima Sharma, is from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Rohit has a younger brother named Vikas Sharma. Wikipedia He is married to Ritika Sajdeh, and they have two children: a daughter named Samaira, born on December 30, 2018, and a son named Ahaan, born on November 15, 2024.
